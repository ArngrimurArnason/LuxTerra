What is Lux Terra?

Welcome to LuxTerra, where real estate meets innovation. Founded by passionate professionals with a shared vision, LuxTerra was built to simplify the way you explore, list, and discover properties in Iceland and beyond. Our mission is to connect people with the places that matter most — whether you're buying your first home, expanding your investment portfolio, or finding the perfect commercial space. We believe in transparency, design-driven experiences, and a personal touch in every interaction. Behind LuxTerra is a team that understands both the industry and the community. Our platform combines local insight with modern tools to bring you listings that are reliable, up-to-date, and tailored to your needs.
